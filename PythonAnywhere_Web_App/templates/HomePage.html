SMS Taxi

How to txt A Cab

  1. Send an SMS to (916) 249-0575 with:

    your pickup location; destination;

    number of passengers in your group;

    F if all passengers are female and would prefer to ride in an all-female taxi; otherwise M

    For example,
    38.538291,-121.726667; 38.540878,-121.737184; 1; M

    Or using an address and restaurant name,
    1213 Alvarado Avenue, Davis, CA; In N Out Davis, CA; 1; M

  2. That's it!
